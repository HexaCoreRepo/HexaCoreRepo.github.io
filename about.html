About Us

HexaCore strives to push iOS customization to the next level with advanced development techniques that continue to enable technology like Super Resolution and Advanced Feature Support. Our themes continue to push the boundaries of what's possible with features like System Sounds, Custom Docks, and even Integrated Wallpapers: Wallpaper's right in your devices' Settings.


We also believe that the packages available on every repository are an integral part to the success of the jailbreak community - especially as features continue to be implemented natively. To support the community, we have made it our goal to stay ahead of the curve. For every feature implemented natively, we aim to have more new features readily available to take its place. This is only possible when we know what the community wants, which is why we strongly encourage user feedback.


It is our mission to ensure that everyone can enjoy these experiences, so we will never charge a fee of any kind on any of our packages. If you have paid for any of the packages on this repo, please report it here.
