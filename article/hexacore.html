IceCool Tech Repo Now HexaCore

December 27th, 2022

Introducing the all-new HexaCore Repo. Designed for the future of iOS Theming.


When IceCool Tech Repo released a couple of years ago, it served a single purpose: to allow our packages to be downloaded hassle free. Just add the repo and download the package.


It was designed to be familiar, straightforward, and accessible with few options to choose from. But, as time went on, IceCool Tech Repo introduced more packages and grew in popularity.


This is when it became time for something new. IceCool Tech Repo needed a complete redesign, but it needed much more than that.


Today, IceCool Tech Repo becomes that something.


IceCool Tech Repo not only gets a complete face lift to bring it into 2023, but it has been entirely recoded and is now more useful than ever before. With so much changed, IceCool Tech Repo is now completely unrecognizable. We decided this was the perfect time for a new name.


So with that said, lets take a look at what has changed with the all-new HexaCore Repo.

Main Page

The content on the Main Page is the first thing you see. So, of course it should look good. This is why HexaCore is full of color - and images.


Starting at the top, HexaCore now has a sleek navigation bar for easy access to popular areas of interest. Like most websites, we have also integrated “home” functionality within our logo. So, when your diving into HexaCore’s content, you no longer have to use your browsers back arrows. Just tap or click our stylized hexagon to return home.


Our Popular Theme preview beautifully adjusts its size depending on the width of your device and just under that, you can now choose from one of our most popular themes right from the Main Page to view in its all-new Store Page.

Store Page

Our themes now get their own Store Pages that better represent the theme itself. Each page displays more information about the package you’re viewing.

Newsroom

Visit HexaCore’s brand new Newsroom for all of the latest updates. News can range from HexaCore updates all the way to theme updates or availability.



This is just a sample of what’s new. You can do even more like add our repo in the new About Us page, keep up to date with website updates in Changelogs, use our FAQ page to answer your questions, and so much more.


We have so many big plans for HexaCore Repo and cannot wait to share them with you.


Here’s to the future of jailbreaking!

~IceCool Tech
