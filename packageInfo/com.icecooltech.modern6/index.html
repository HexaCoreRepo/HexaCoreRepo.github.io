What makes Modern 6 so special?

Modern 6 is truly a one of a kind iOS 6 theme. It’s not only the closest you’ll get to iOS 6, but it also does all the hard work for you so you can save hours of your time from trying out different iOS 6 tweaks and themes. Modern 6 is also fully customizable and allows you to manually enable or disable specific features at any time you like.


Modern 6 includes:

  • Over 115 iOS 6 app icons designed to look beautiful on any display
  • Full set of iOS 6 Settings icons with support for dark mode
  • Entire collection of iOS 6 wallpapers right in the Settings app
  • System sounds taken directly from iOS 6 for a truly nostalgic experience
  • True iOS 6 app badges with proper shading and fonts
  • iOS 6 app labels with proper shading and fonts
  • iOS 6 dock with native reflections*
  • iOS 6 boot logo brings the nostalgia to every respring
