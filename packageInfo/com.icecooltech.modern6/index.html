Modern 6 brings the user over 100 iOS 6 app icons as well as the full set of iOS 6 Settings icons. Every app and Settings icon are all incredibly sharp and are intentionally built to look amazing on all of the current iPhone displays. *Original icon refers to an app icon present pre-2013 (iOS 7) post 2011 (iOS 5).
